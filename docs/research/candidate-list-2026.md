## Research gaps

- **`translation-language`:** Few vendors publish a durable, self-serve **individual student** discount; most edu pricing is **sales-led** or bundled (Google/Microsoft).
- **`legal`:** Meaningful access is almost always via **law school IDs** or trialsвЂ”not DTC student checkout.
- **`image-design` / `video`:** Many generative tools (**Midjourney**, **HeyGen**) explicitly **lack** student discounts; only bulk/ambassador programs.
- **`learning-education`:** Consumer study apps often use **promos** rather than stable official edu pagesвЂ”high churn.
- **Regional lock-in:** **Gemini student**, **Colab edu**, **Cursor**, **Copilot** pausesвЂ”US-centric or time-limited in 2026.
- **Third-party verify:** StudentBeans, UNiDAYS, SheerIDвЂ”note in YAML `proof_required`, donвЂ™t use aggregator URLs as `source_url` unless no official page exists.
- **Institution vs individual:** ChatGPT Edu, Claude for Education, Lexis+ AI, Hugging Face Academia Hub need clear `eligibility` text to avoid misleading individual students.
